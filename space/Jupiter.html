Welcome to Jupiter!

Jupiter is the fifth planet from our sun and the largest in the entire solar system!
Jupiter is classified as a gas giant because it is entirely composed of gas, much like the other gas giants Saturn, Neptune, and Uranus.
Jupiter can be seen with the naked eye, it is the third brightest object in the night sky. It was known to the ancient Romans, who named it after their god Jupiter.

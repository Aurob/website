Welcome to the Sun!

The Sun is the star at the center of the Solar System. It is seen in the sky and gives light to the Earth.
The Sun is a star like many others in our Milky Way galaxy. It has existed for a little over 4.5 billion years, and is going to continue for at least as long.
It has a mass of 1.989 x 10^30 kg, which is 333,000 times the mass of the Earth. The Earth can also fit inside the Sun 1.3 million times!

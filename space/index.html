Welcome to Earth

Earth is the third planet from the sun and the largest terrestial planet in the solar system!
It formed around 4.5 billion years ago and is the only planet in the solar system known to harbour life!
Earth is home to over one million different animal species and arguably the most interesting of them is the Homo sapien!
There are roughly 7 billion humans living on Earth right now and the amazing thing about them is that they've sent objects away from Earth to explore outer space!
